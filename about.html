Hi There, I’m Kira!

A Computer Engineering Student Exploring Tech, Hardware, and How Things Work

My journey into tech started in the most unexpected way—I found a breadboard on the floor of my high school. Someone had dropped it, and I couldn’t find the owner, so out of curiosity, I held onto it and started asking my engineering friend about what it was and what it did. That spark of curiosity stuck with me.

When I got to college, I began as a Computer Science major but quickly realized I was missing the hands-on, physical side of tech. That’s when I switched to Computer Engineering—and I’ve been loving it ever since. Now I spend my time learning about cybersecurity, embedded systems, and hardware design.

Outside of school, I’m deeply into fiber arts. I’ve been crocheting since I was 12, and now I’m learning to quilt. Lately I’ve been especially drawn to color—the way it interacts, tells stories, and shapes emotion—and you’ll definitely see that influence throughout this site.

This website is a mix of everything I care about—tech, design, learning, and storytelling. Thanks for stopping by -Kira ⭐️🪲🌀
